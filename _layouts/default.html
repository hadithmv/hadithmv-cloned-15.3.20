About

           

Hi there, welcome to Hadithmv.

The accessibility to Hadith via web has changed the muslim world. Atleast for most english speaking audiences. Sadly, maldivians have no such web resource in their native language 'Dhivehi'. If you want to help change this situation for the better, join us in this rewarding journey to make dhivehi translations of hadith available to all by the will of Allah.
