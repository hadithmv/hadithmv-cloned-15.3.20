So that those who were to be destroyed (for their rejecting the Faith) might be destroyed after a clear evidence, and those who were to live (in Faith) might live after a clear evidence. And indeed, Allah is the Hearing, the Knowing.


. . .


[8:42]


: : :


Abu Moosa al-Ashari narrated: The Prophet said: The example of the guidance and knowledge that Allah has sent me with, is like abundant rain that fell upon the earth. So from that land was good (fertile soil) that accepted (and absorbed) the water. So it grew lots of pasture and grass. And from that land was hard (soil) that held the water (for a while without absorbing it all up quickly). So Allah made the people benefit from it, and they drank it, made (their animals) drink from it, and irrigated their plants with it. And that water also fell upon another piece of land, which was only bare and barren. It could neither hold water nor grow pasture. So (the first) is the example of someone who understands Allah's religion and what Allah has sent me with (ie: the Quran and Sunnah), and so he learns (it himself) and teaches (it to others). And (the last) is the example of someone who did not even raise his head with it (ie: did not even bother with it), and did not accept the guidance of Allah that I have been sent with (ie: the Quran and Sunnah).
